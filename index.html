Changjie Lu

I am a senior undergraduate student at the Department of Applied Mathematics, Wenzhou-Kean University, advised by Prof. Gaurav Gupta.

My current research lies in causal representation learning for medical image analysis and developing deep learning technology to solve traditional problems in biomedical science, such as 3D image reconstruction of macromolecular proteins and quantifying the left atrial scar. Besides, I am studying robust and lightweight algorithms for real-world applications (e.g., image deraining, deblurring, and low light enhancement.).

I am looking for a PhD position in 2023 fall. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to drop me an email.
